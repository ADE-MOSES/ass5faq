Frequently Asked Questions

How often should the engine oil of a car be replaced?

Engine oil should be replaced based of the type of oil, mileage covered or durationof use.

Should my radiator fan run always?

No. Radiator fans are meant to reduce heat generated by the engine. If the engine is below the operating temperature, the fan does not need to run..

What does the letter L stand for on the gear selector?

This L letter represent the lower torque of the gear and it is mainly used when driving up hill or towing another vehicle
